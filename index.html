Iceland
Iceland, a Nordic island nation, is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, vibrant culture, and unique geographical features. Situated in the North Atlantic Ocean, it is the most sparsely populated country in Europe, with a population of around 366,000 people. The capital and largest city is Reykjavik, which is also the northernmost capital of a sovereign state in the world.
New Zealand
New Zealand, an island nation in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, is celebrated for its diverse landscapes, rich Maori culture, and vibrant cities. Comprising two main landmasses, the North Island and the South Island, along with over 700 smaller islands, New Zealand has a population of about 5 million people. The capital city is Wellington, while Auckland is the largest and most populous urban area.
Switzerland
Switzerland, a landlocked country in Central Europe, is famed for its picturesque landscapes, high standard of living, and robust economy. Bordered by Germany, France, Italy, Austria, and Liechtenstein, it is known for its neutrality, multilingualism, and as a global hub for finance and diplomacy. The country has a population of approximately 8.5 million people, with Bern as its capital and Zurich as the largest city.
Finland
Finland, a Nordic country in Northern Europe, is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, high standard of living, and innovative society. With a population of approximately 5.5 million people, it is one of the most sparsely populated countries in Europe. The capital and largest city is Helsinki.
Greenland
Greenland, an autonomous territory within the Kingdom of Denmark, is the world's largest island, located between the Arctic and Atlantic Oceans. Known for its stunning icy landscapes, vast glaciers, and remote communities, Greenland is sparsely populated, with a population of around 56,000 people. The capital and largest city is Nuuk.
Poland
Poland, located in Central Europe, is a country with a rich history, vibrant culture, and diverse landscapes. With a population of over 38 million people, it is one of the largest countries in the region. The capital and largest city is Warsaw.
Ireland
Ireland, an island nation in the North Atlantic, is known for its lush landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant cities. The country is divided into the Republic of Ireland, which occupies most of the island, and Northern Ireland, which is part of the United Kingdom. The Republic of Ireland has a population of about 5 million people, with Dublin as its capital and largest city.
Thailand
Thailand, located in Southeast Asia, is known for its vibrant culture, stunning beaches, and rich history. With a population of over 69 million people, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. The capital city is Bangkok.
